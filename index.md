## Short Bio

Yi Zhao is currently a Ph.D. student at [AGI Institute](https://cs.sjtu.edu.cn/en/yjjg/813.html) of [Shanghai Jiaotong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/en/) under the guidance of Prof. [Hai Zhao](https://bcmi.sjtu.edu.cn/home/zhaohai/), co-advised by Associate Researcher [Zuchao Li](https://zcli-charlie.github.io/). He obtained his B.E. and M.E. degrees in [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://www.hit.edu.cn/en/) in 2020 and 2023, respectively.

His research primarily focuses on system-algorithm co-design, particularly on efficient methods to accelerate LLM inference, including prompt compression ([DAC](https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.952/)), efficient attention mechanisms ([IAM](https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.959/)), KV cache compression ([SmallKV](https://neurips.cc/virtual/2025/loc/san-diego/poster/120344)), collaborative reasoning ([TrigReason](https://openreview.net/forum?id=HrpRxHHd57)), and combining speculative decoding with sparse attention (SmallInfer).
